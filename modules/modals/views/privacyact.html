Privacy Act Statement

This statement serves to inform you of the purpose for collecting personal information required by the Command Central system and how it will be used.

  • Principal Purposes: To obtain information from individuals to allow the access, update and view of personal information on the Command Central website.
  • Routine Uses: To access, update and view a Service Member's Command Central profile. Information entered and stored may be disclosed as generally permitted under 5 U.S.C of the Privacy Act, as amended.
  • Disclosure: Voluntary. However, if you choose not to provide or update your information, the absence of the requested information will result in your not being able to access and use services through the website.
